Classic Lasagna Recipe

This classic lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food. Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese blend together in perfect harmony. It's a family favorite that's sure to please!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and fresh basil. Mix until well blended.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  12. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
